Our Company History

Founded in 2013, Robs Investments Holdings has grown from a boutique financial advisory firm to a comprehensive wealth management company serving over 500 clients. What started as Rob's vision to provide personalized, ethical investment advice has evolved into a full-service financial institution that helps individuals and families achieve their long-term financial goals.

Our firm manages over R50K in assets across diverse portfolios, featuring strategic investments in equities, bonds, real estate, and alternative investments. We pride ourselves on our disciplined approach to risk management, comprehensive financial planning, and creating personalized investment strategies for clients of all backgrounds and financial situations.

Our Vision

To be the premier investment firm that empowers individuals and families to achieve financial independence through innovative investment strategies, personalized service, and unwavering commitment to ethical practices.
