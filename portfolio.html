Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum investment amount?
Our minimum investment varies by package: Basic Package starts at R2,500, Growth Package at R10,000, Premium Package at R50,000, and Family Legacy Package at R100,000.
How often will I receive portfolio updates?
Update frequency depends on your package: Basic clients receive quarterly reviews, Growth clients get monthly updates, Premium clients receive weekly monitoring, and Family Legacy clients get continuous oversight.
Can I change my investment package later?
Yes, you can upgrade your package at any time as your investment grows or your needs change. We'll help you transition smoothly to a higher service level.
What fees do you charge?
Our wealth management fee is 1.25% annually for managed portfolios. Consultation services range from R150-R300 per hour depending on complexity. All fees are transparent and disclosed upfront.
How do I get started?
Simply click on the "Get Started" link for your preferred package, or contact us directly to schedule your initial consultation. We'll guide you through the entire process.
